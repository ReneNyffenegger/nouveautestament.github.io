ἀμιξία, Ion. ἀμιξ-ίη, ἡ, a being ἄμικτος, and so,     I. purity, Thphr.CP4.16.2.     II. of persons, want of intercourse, ἀλλήλων Th.1.3; πρὸς ἅπαντας Luc.Tim.42; unsociableness, Isoc.6.67; ἀμιξίη χρημάτων want of commercial dealings, Hdt.2.136; cf. ἀμειξία.     2. abstinence from sexual intercourse, Aristaenet.2.3.
