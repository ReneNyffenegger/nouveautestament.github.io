στέγος, εος, τό, = τέγος, prop.roof, LXXEp.Je.10 (v.l. τέγους), D.S.19.45, IG5(1).1114.14 (Geronthrae), Poll.1.81, Lib.Or.11.162, and so perh. in E.IT48; ἐπὶ τὸ αὐτὸ σ. ἐλθεῖν 'under the same roof', SIG 1179.20 (Cnidus).     II. mostly, like στέγη, house, mansion, A.Pers.141 (anap.), Ag.310, S.Aj.307, etc.; prob. in OGI619.5 (Syria, iv A.D.).     III. δέξαι μ’ ἐς τὸ σὸν σ., i.e. into the urn containing his ashes, S.El.1165; grave, Lyc.1098.     IV. brothel, στεγέεσσι Man.2.430, cf. 6.533.
