κυκλόθεν, Adv. from all around, κ. ὁδὸς περίχει Lys.7.28, cf. Hp.Fract.33, Thphr.HP4.6.10 (dub.l.), etc.: c. gen., LXX3 Ki.18.32, al., Apoc.4.3: c. dat., LXX3 Ki.6.5: spelt κύκλωθεν, IPE12.175 (Olbia), and sts. in codd., but this spelling is condemned by Theognost.Can.156, and arose from a supposed connexion with κύκλῳ.
