συνᾰγων-ιστής, οῦ, Dor. συνᾰγωνισ-τάς, ὁ, one who shares with another in a contest, fellow-combatant, Pl.Alc.1.119d, Isoc.4.142, etc.; τινος for a thing, Aeschin.2.183, 3.89, D.18.41; accomplice, confederate, Hyp.Ath.3; πρός τι Plb.10.34.2.     2. οἱ σ. those who take part in contending for the prize, Michel 1016 (Teos, ii B.C.); τραγικοὶ σ. OGI51.56 (Ptolemais, iii B.C.); of a company of Dionysiac artists, CIG3082 (Teos).     3. generally, fellow-worker, POxy.1676.36 (iii A.D.).
