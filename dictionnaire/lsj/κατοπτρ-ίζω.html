κατοπτρ-ίζω, show as in a mirror or by reflexion, τοῦ -ίζοντος [τὴν ἶριν] ἀστέρος Placit.3.5.11:—Pass., to be mirrored, Anon.Oxy.1609.19.     II. Med., look into a mirror, behold oneself in it, Zeno Stoic.1.66, S.E.P.1.48, Ath.15.687c, etc.     2. behold as in a mirror, ἰδέαν Ph.1.107; δόξαν Κυρίου 2 Ep.Cor.3.18 (but here perh.reflect).
