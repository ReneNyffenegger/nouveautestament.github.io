κῠν-άς, άδος, pecul. fem. of κύνεος, of a dog, ἡμέραι κ. the dog-days, Plu.2.380d.     II. mostly as Subst.,     1. (sc. θρίξ), dog's hair, of a bad fleece, Theoc.15.19.     2. = κυνάρα, Hsch.     3. among the Spartans, = ἀπομαγδαλιά (q.v.), Polem.Hist.77, Poll.6.93.     4. kind of nail, Sch.Od.7.91, Eust.1570.48.
