κᾰλαῦροψ, οπος, ἡ, shepherd's staff or crook, which was thrown so as to drive back the cattle to the herd, Il.23.845, Antim.61, A.R.2.33, AP6.106 (Zonas), APl.4.74, D.H.1.39, Longus1.8, BSA16.107 (Pamphylia, ii/iii A.D.). (Prob. καλά-ϝροψ, from *ϝρέπω, v. ῥέπω: but the first part of the word is uncertain.)
