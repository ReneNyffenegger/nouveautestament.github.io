ἄμοτον, Adv. insatiably, incessantly, in Hom. always with Verbs expressing passion, desire, etc., esp. ἄ. μεμαώς full of insatiate longing, Il.4.440, al.; ἄ. κλαίω τεθνηότα I weep continually, 19.300; ἄ. κεχολωμένος implacably angered, 23.567; μάχης ἄ. μενεαίνων Hes.Sc.361; ἡμίονοι ἄ. τανύοντο they struggled restlessly forwards, Od.6.83: later, vehemently, violently, λὶς ἄ. κεραΐζει Theoc.25.202; but στῆ ῥ’ ἄ. stood unwaveringly, A.R.2.78:—later regul. Adv. ἀμό-τως Sch.Il.4.410.     II. later, Adj. ἄμοτος, ον, furious, savage, κακόν prob. in Simon.37.16; θήρ Theoc.25.242; πῦρ Mosch.4.104.—Poet. word.
