ὁλκ-αῖος, α, ον, (ὁλκή) drawn along, towed, of a ship (cf. ὁλκάς), Nic.Th.268: hence, trailing, dragging, σειρή, of a serpent, ib.119; ἀτραπός ib.160; κακά Lyc.216.     II. as Subst. ὁλκαία, Ion. ὁλκαίη, ἡ, tail, because it is trailed along, Nic.Th.123, 225, A.R.4.1614 (v.l. ἀλκαία, which Schn. writes in Nic. ll. cc.).
