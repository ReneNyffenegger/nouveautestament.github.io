ἐρώτ-ημα, ατος, τό, that which is asked, question, Th.3.54, etc. ; ἡ πρὸς τὸ ἐ. ἀπόκρισις ib.60; τὰ ἐ. τοῦ ξυνθήματος asking for the password, Id.7.44; ἐ. περί τινος Pl.Prt.336d; ἐ. ἐρέσθαι, Id.Phlb.42e; διπλᾶ ἔστρεφε τὰ -ήματα Id.Euthd.276d.     2. in Stoic terminology, a question requiring the answer 'Yes' or 'No', opp. πύσμα, Chrysipp.Stoic.2.61.     II. in Dialectic, question inviting an answer which may help to refute an opponent, Arist.APr.64a36 (pl.), APo77a36, al.
