*ἵλημι [ῑ], = foreg., only in imper. ἵληθι, in prayers, be gracious! Od.3.380, 16.184, h.Hom.20.8, etc.: Aeol. ἔλλᾱθι (q. v.): Dor. ἵλᾰθι Theoc.15.143, Luc.Epigr.22; both together, ἵλᾰθ’, ἄναξ, ἵληθι AP12.158 (Mel.): pl., ἵλᾰτε A.R.4.984, Man.6.754. (Prob. pf. imper. fr. se-sl-; Aeol. ἔλλ- points to εἵλ- as the true Ion. spelling; Dor. pf. part. dat. ἱλαότι (leg. εἱλ-) Hsch.: ῑ is genuine in ἵλεως, ἱλάσκομαι [fr. si-sl-].)
