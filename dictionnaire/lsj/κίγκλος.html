κίγκλος, ὁ, prob. dabchick, Podiceps ruficollis, Ar.Fr.29, Autocr.1, Anaxandr.41.66, Arist.HA593b5: prov., κίγκλου πτωχότερος 'poor as a church mouse', because it had no nest of its own, Men.221, cf. Ael.NA12.9:—Suid. has κίγκαλος, but κιγκάλους is unmetrical as a fish-name in Numen. ap. Ath.7.326a.
