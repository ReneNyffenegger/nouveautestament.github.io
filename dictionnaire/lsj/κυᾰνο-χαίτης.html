κυᾰνο-χαίτης, ου, ὁ, dark-haired, in Hom. usu. of Poseidon, perh. in reference to the dark blue of the sea, Il.20.144, Od.9.536, cf. Hes.Th.278; Ἀρείων Thebais4; of a horse, dark-maned, Il.20.224, Hes.Sc.120: voc. κυανοχαῖτα h.Cer.347:—also nom. κυανοχαῖτα Il.13.563, 14.390; treated as indeclin. and joined with dat., κυανοχαῖτα Ποσειδάωνι Antim.27.
