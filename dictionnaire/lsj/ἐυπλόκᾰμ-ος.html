ἐυπλόκᾰμ-ος, Ep.ἐϋπλ-όκᾰμος, ον, with goodly locks, fair-haired, epith. of goddesses and women, in Hom., etc., esp. of Eos and Artemis, Od.5.390,20.80, cf. B.3.34, etc.; later also of boys and men, Mosch.1.12, Orph.L.439; εὐ. κόμαι goodly tresses, E.IA790 (lyr.): metaph., ἐϋπλοκάμου πολιῆς ἁλός Archil.11, cf. Opp.C.2.131; of the tentacles of polypi, ib.3.182.
