Call.Fr.162, AP6.220.15 (Diosc.); rare in Prose, Demetr.Lac. Herc.1014.49; also, offerings to Aesculapius and other gods for recovery from illness, IG14.967a5: c. gen., νούσων ibid.; ζ. ἀποθύειν Ael.NA11.31: sg. in Orac. ap. Plu.Arat.53—a form ζώγρια, τά, Suid.—Adj. ζωάγριος, ον, ζ. μοι χάριτας ὀφλήσεις you will owe me thanks for a life saved, Babr.50.15.
