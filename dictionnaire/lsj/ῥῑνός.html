Il.16.636 and ῥινόν 2.     3. pl., thongs of boxing-gloves, A.R.2.58.     4. leather of a sling, AP7.172 (Antip. Sid.).—The gender is fem. in Il.7.248, 20.276, Od.22.278 (v.l.), Hes.Sc.152, E.l.c., Nic.Th.361, A.R.4.174; masc. in Nic.Al.476, Opp.C.3.277: cf. ῥινόν, τό.
