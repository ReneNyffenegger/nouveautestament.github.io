μογ-ερός, ά, όν, also ός, όν Nic.Al.419 (v.l. for σμυγερός): poet. Adj. used by Trag. in anap. and lyr. (μόγος, cf. σμογερός):     I. of persons, toiling, distressed, wretched, A.Pr.565, Th.827, E.Tr.783, 790, Ar.Ach.1207; so μ. οἶκοι S.El.93. Adv. μογε-ρῶς Man.1.146.     II. of things, toilsome, grievous, κάματοι v.l. in AP7.508 (Emp. or Simon.); ἄχεα E.Med.205; ἀκουαί ear trouble, Marc.Sid.86.
