μετεωρ-ισμός, ὁ, = μετεώρισις I, Hp.Prog.9 (pl.); τῶν ποδῶν Arist.IA711b23; τοῦ ὅλου σώματος ib.713a23; rising to the surface, of roots, Thphr.CP1.3.5.     II. being raised up: hence, swelling, Hp.Art.50.     2. μ. γνώμης mental trouble or disturbance, Id.Acut. (Sp.)14 (γνώμης is prob. interpol.), cf. Vett.Val.185.20 (pl.); wild thinking, vain imagining, Metrod.Herc.831.4, 12 (pl.).     3. delay, procrastination, PMasp.32.55 (vi A.D.).
