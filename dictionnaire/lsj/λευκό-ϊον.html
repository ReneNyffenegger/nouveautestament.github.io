λευκό-ϊον [ῐ], τό, for λευκὸν ἴον, lit. white-violet:     I. gilliflower, Matthiola incana, Theoc.7.64, Dsc.3.123, etc.     II. snowdrop, Galanthus nivalis, flowering very early, Thphr.HP6.8.1; joined with the narcissus and lily in AP5.143 (Mel.), 146 (Id.).     III. λ. τὸ μέλαν, = ἴον τὸ μέλαν, Hp.Nat.Mul.32.
