βράκαι, ῶν, αἱ, Lat.braccae, breeches, trews, worn by the Gauls, D.S.5.30 (βράκες (sic): ἀναξυρίδες, Hsch.):—Dim. βράκια, τά, Sch.Ar.V.1082, PGiss.80.6 (iv A. D.), IG5(1).1406.23 (Edict.Diocl., Asine):— hence βρακάριος, ὁ, breeches-maker, ib.18, cf. POxy.1341 (iv A. D.):— but βρακαρίαι, αἱ, breeches, PGiss.90.6 (ii A. D.).
