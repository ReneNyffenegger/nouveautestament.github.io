σπεκουλάτωρ, ορος, ὁ, = Lat. speculator, prop. scout: but in the Roman Imperial army,     1. one of the principales or head-quarters' staff of a legionary commander or provincial governor (whose duties included the carrying out of executions), Ev.Marc.6.27, POxy.1193.1 (iv A.D.), etc.     2. one of the Imperial body-guard (speculatores Augusti), = δορυφόρος, Suid.
