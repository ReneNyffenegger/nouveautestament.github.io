συναίρ-εσις, εως, ἡ, taking or drawing together, ἡ τῶν ἄκρων εἰς ταὐτὸ σ. Longin.10.3; ς. (sc. καρπῶν) Ath.11.489f; contraction, closing, τῶν διοδευθησομένων χωρίων Sor.2.59.     2. contraction of front, opp. αὔξησις, Ascl.Tact.11.7; shortening, reduction of an estimated distance, Ptol.Geog.1.8.3; τοῦ μήκους (of a fractured limb) Sor.Fract.19 (συνερεισις cod.).     3. aggregation, Dam.Pr.96; synthesis, ib.277,280; concentration, τῆς νοερᾶς οὐσίας Simp.in Ph.635.32; generalization, opp. διαίρεσις, Eliasin Porph.76.19.     4. in Gramm., synaeresis, whereby two vowels are not changed, but coalesce into a diphthong, as ὀϊστός, οἰστός, opp. διαίρεσις, Quint.Inst.1.5.17, A.D.Adv.132.25; but also contraction, as of κύημα to κῦμα, Gal.6.642.
