ἤτριον, Dor. ἄτριον, τό, warp (the woof being κρόκη), Pl.Phdr.268a, Theoc.18.33, AP6.288 (Leon., pl.): in pl., a thin, fine cloth, such that one could see between the threads, ἤτρια πέπλων E.Ion1421; ἤτρια βύβλων leaves made of strips of papyrus, prob. cj. in AP9.350 (Leon.Alex.); τὸ διὰ ἠτρίου ἠθημένον Gal.19.98.
