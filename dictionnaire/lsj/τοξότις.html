τοξότις, ιδος, ἡ, fem. of foreg., archeress, of Atalanta, Call.Dian.223; of Artemis, Orph.H.36.2, etc.: as Adj., τ. χείρ AP6.188 (Leon.):—Τοξότιδες, name of a play by A.     II. loophole for shooting arrows, Plb.8.7.3; cf. τοξικός, I.3.     III. a plant, = ἀρτεμισία, Poet.de herb.26, Apul.Herb.10; also called τοξιτησία, Ps.-Dsc.3.113.
