Ὄλβια, τά, older form for Ἄλπια, the Alps, Posidon.48 J.; Ὄλπια in EM623.1.     II. Ὀλβία, ἡ, name of several cities, of which the best known was the Milesian colony in Scythia, SIG286.15 (iv B. C.), Str.7.3.17, etc. : its citizens being Ὀλβιοπολῖται, Hdt.4.18, SIG 286.1: hence Adj. Ὀλβιοπολῑτικός, ή, όν, ib.218.16.
