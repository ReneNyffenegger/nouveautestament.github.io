ἄμητος [ᾱ], ὁ, (ἀμάω A) reaping, harvesting, Il.19.223.     2. harvest, harvest-time, Hes.Op.384,575, Hdt.2.14, 4.42, Hp.Epid.6.8.19, Thphr.HP3.4.4, A.R.3.418, etc.     II. crop, harvest gathered in, or field when reaped, D.P.194, Arat.1097: with another Subst., ληΐοιο ἀμήτοιο Opp.C.1.527: metaph., of a beard, AP11.368 (Jul.). (Gramm. distinguish ἄμητος (I) from ἀμητός (II), the latter being regarded as Adj. (sc. σῖτος), cf. Hdn.Gr.1.220, but Ammon. reverses the distinction.)
