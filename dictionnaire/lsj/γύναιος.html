γύναιος [ῠ], α, ον, = γυναικεῖος, γ. δῶρα presents made to a woman, Od.11.521.15.247; φυὴ γυναίη Mosch.2.45.     II. Subst. γύναιον, τό, little woman, term of endearment for a wife, Ar.V.610, Th.792: more freq. in a contemptuous sense, weak woman, And.1.130, etc.; γυναίου πρᾶγμ’ ἐποίει D.25.57, cf. Arist.EN1171b10: but simply, = γυνή, Aen.Tact.2.6, D.S.17.24, J.AJ1.12.4, al., Ph.1.99, al., Plu.Pel.9.
