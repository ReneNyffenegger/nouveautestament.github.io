Antipho Soph.15; τὸ ἔ. their living and growing, of trees, Ael.VH13.1.     2. ἡ ἔ. ὑγρότης the moisture necessary to life, Thphr.CP1.1.3; αἷμα ἔ. τῇ γῇ πινόμενον Philostr.Im.1.24.     II. lasting one's whole life, ἔ. τιμωρία D.C.78.12.     III. ἔ. γενέσθαι recover consciousness after a swoon, Longus2.30.
