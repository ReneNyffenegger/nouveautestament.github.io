περίπολος, ον, (περιπέλομαι) going the rounds, patrolling: hence, as Subst.,     1. watchman, patrol, Epich.35.10, Plu.Num.16, Luc.VH2.6, etc.; π. τῶν βασιλείων Lib.Or.59.144.     2. pl., at Athens, patrol, in which ἔφηβοι (and also non-Athenians, cf. Lys.13.71 with Th.8.92) served, Ar.Av.1177, Th.4.67, 8.92; τοὺς π. ἀπιέν’ εἰς τὰ φρούρια Eup.341: sg. in Aeschin.2.167.     3. generally, attendant, follower, as fem., S.Ant.1150 (lyr.).     4. π. πάσης τῆς ὑφ’ ἥλιον 'globe-trotter', Him.Or.14.25, cf. Lib.Ep.168.     5. περίπολος (sc. ναῦς), ἡ, guardship, Arr.An.2.20.2.
