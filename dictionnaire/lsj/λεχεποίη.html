λεχεποίη, ἡ, (λέχος, ποία) grown with grass fit to make a bed, i.e. grassy, meadowy, epith. of various towns, Il.2.697, h.Ap.224, h.Merc.88:—masc. λεχεποίης, ου, of the river Asopus, from its grassy banks, Il.4.383, Orac. ap. Hdt.9.43.—Hom. has both forms in acc. sg. only; the dat. occurs ap.Hdt.l.c.
