στῆριγξ, ιγγος, ἡ, support, prop, stay, σ. τοῦ σώματος, of the κνήμη or large bone of the leg, X.Eq.1.5; αἱ σ. [τῶν πύργων] D.S.18.70.     b. = παρακερκίς I, Poll.2.191 (pl.).     2. fork with which the shaft or pole of a two-wheeled chariot was propped, until the beasts were yoked to it, Lys.Fr.330 S., Plu.2.280f: acc. στήριγγᾰν Maiuri Nuova Silloge48 (Rhodes).
