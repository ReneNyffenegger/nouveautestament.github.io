σοῦσον, τό, lily, = κρίνον, Aristobulus and Chares ap.Ath.12.513f, dub. in Arist.Mir.838a23 (Phoen.and Phrygian word, acc. to Zonar.; cf. Hebr. shûshan; σασά Syrian acc. to Ps.-Dsc.3.102).     II. Σοῦσα, τά, Susa, in the province of Susiana or Shushan, Hdt.1.188; the winter and spring residence of the King of Persia, Id.5.52, X.Cyr.8.6.22, An.3.5.15:—Σούσιος, ὁ, a man of Susa, Id.Cyr.5.1.2:—Σουσίς, ίδος, ἡ, the province of Susa, A.Pers.119,557 (s.v.l.) (Σ. γυνή a woman of Susa, X.Cyr.4.6.11); also Σουσιάδες πέτραι D.S.17.68; and Σουσιανή, ἡ, Id.2.2. (Derived from σοῦσον acc. to St.Byz. (σοῦσαν codd.).)
