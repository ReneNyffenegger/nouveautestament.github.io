ἀτριβ-ής, ές, not rubbed: hence,     1. of places, not traversed, pathless, Th.4.8,29, Ph.2.257, al.; of roads, not worn or used, X.An.4.2.8, App.Hisp.62: generally, fresh, new, X.Mem.4.3.13, cf. Cyr.8.7.22 (v.l. ἀκρ-).     2. of the neck, not galled, Pl.Amat.134b; ἀ. ζεύγλης Babr.37.1.     II. not practised in, πολεμικῶν ἀγώνων D.H.3.52. Adv. ἀτρι-βῶς Poll.5.145.
