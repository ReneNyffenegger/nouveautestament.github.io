ὑποβῐβ-άζω, Causal of ὑποβαίνω, draw or bring down: Medic., carry off downwards, i. e. by purging, ὑ. τὰ χολώδη Dsc.3.30, cf. Antyll. ap. Orib.6.6.1.     II. Med., stoop or crouch down, of a horse that lowers itself to take up the rider, X.Eq.6.16, Poll.1.213; cf. ὑπόβασις II.     III. lower, humble, Hsch., Phot., Suid.     IV. Music., transpose lower, opp. ὑπερβ., Theo Sm.p.92 H.     V. Pass., of numbers, to be in a descending series, Iamb.in Nic.p.53 P.
