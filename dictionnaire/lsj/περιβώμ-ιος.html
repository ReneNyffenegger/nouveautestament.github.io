περιβώμ-ιος, ον, round the altar, Suid. s.v. ἔλεγος: Subst., τὰ π. images placed about the altar, LXX2 Ch.34.3:—but περιβωμόῳ (sic), space round a βωμός, Rev.Hist.Rel.97.275; περιβώμιοι, οἱ, functionaries attached to Asiatic cults, IG12(3).1126 (Melos): hence Peribomius, name of an Archigallus, Juv.2.16, cf.Sch.ad loc.
