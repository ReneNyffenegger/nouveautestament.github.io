χορδ-αψός, ὁ (on the accent v. Hdn.Gr.1.227), a disease in the great guts, the same as εἰλεός in the small ones, Hp.Coac.502; but the meanings are reversed by Diocl. (Fr.73) ap.Cels.4.20.1; an aggravated form of εἰλεός acc. to Aret.SA2.6, but not distd. fr. it by Gal.8.388 (fr. χορδή, ἕψω, acc. to Aret. l. c.).
