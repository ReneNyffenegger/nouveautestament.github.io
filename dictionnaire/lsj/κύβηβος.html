κύβηβος, ον, (κύβη) stooping with the head, EM543.10.     II. Κύβηβος [ῠ], ὁ, minister of Cybele, Semon.36; cf. κυβέβις· γάλλος, κτλ., Hsch.: generally, one ecstatic or frantic, Cratin.82:—hence κῠβηβάω, to be frantic, Phot., Hsch., cf. EM543.11 (-βειν codd.):— Hsch. also has the forms Κυβήκη (Hippon.120) and Κύβηκος.
