ἀνεπί-πληκτος, ον, not liable to be reproved, Eup.397; βίος E.Or.922, Men.Epit.489. Adv. ἀνεπιπλήκ-τως Hsch., f.l. in Ph.2.454.     2. in bad sense, not reproved, licentious, τροφῇ ἀ. τραφῆναι Pl.Lg.695b, cf. Eus.Mynd.62.     II. Act., not reproving or blaming, τὸ ἀ. abstinence from blame or criticism, M.Ant.1.10.
