τῑμ-έω, Dor., = τιμάω, prob. in IG5(1).962.39 (Cotyrta, ii B.C.); inf. τιμεῖν ib.14.952.16 (Acragas, iii B.C.), 5(1).1432.41 (Messene); part. fem. τιμίονσα GDI5150.22 (Crete, found at Delos, ii B.C.); masc. pl. τιμέοντες SIG637.6 (Delph., ii B.C.):—in Hdt.6.39 all codd. have ἐπι-τιμέων; similar forms in the Ionic writings of Luc.(Dom.20, Astr.1).
