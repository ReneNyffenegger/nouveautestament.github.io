Moer. l.c., etc.:—moth, [χρυσὸν] οὐ σ. οὐδὲ κὶς δάπτει Pi.Fr.222, cf. Ar. l. c., Str.13.1.54, Ev.Matt.6.19, Ev.Luc.12.33; attacking books, Luc. l. c.     2. metaph., σῆτες ἀκανθοβάται or -λόγοι, of the Grammarians, bookworms, AP11.322 (Antiphan.), 347 (Phil.).
