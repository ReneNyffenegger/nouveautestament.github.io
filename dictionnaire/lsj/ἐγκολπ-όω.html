ἐγκολπ-όω, make full and round, like the folds of a robe, Orph.A.1183 (tm.):—Pass., ἐγκεκολπῶσθαι to be curved into a bay or bays, Arist.Mu.393a23:—Med., put in the fold of one's robe: hence metaph., 'have in one's pocket', τὴν τοῦ Καίσαρος ἰσχύν D.C.48.52:—Pass., to have folded round one, χιτῶνα ἐνεκεκόλπωτο Id.62.2: metaph., ἀρετήν Mich.in EN603.24.
