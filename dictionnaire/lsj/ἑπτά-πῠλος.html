ἑπτά-πῠλος, ον, with seven gates, epith. of Boeotian Thebes (cf. ἑκατόμπυλος), Il.4.406, Od.11.263, B.18.47, Anaxandr.41.21, cf. A.Th.165 (lyr.), S.Ant.119 (lyr.).     II. ἑ. κλῖμαξ stairway symbolizing the ascent of the soul through the seven planetary spheres, Cels. ap. OrigenesCels.6.22 (prob. for ὑψί-πῠλος).
