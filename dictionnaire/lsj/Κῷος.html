Κῷος, α, ον, of, from the island Κῶς, Coan, IG12.195.7, al., Hdt.7.164, etc.; Κώϊος Call.Fr.254.     II. as Subst. Cael.292a29 (v.l.).     III. Κῷον (sc. ἱμάτιον), τό, a light semi-transparent garment, made at Cos, Hsch.     2. a measure of wine, Ostr.Fay.44 (ii/iii A.D.), BGU531 ii 8: pl. written κόα, Sammelb.7199.2, al. (ii A.D.).     3. = ἐνέχυρον, Hsch. (also κώϊον); cf. κοῖον, κοῦα.
