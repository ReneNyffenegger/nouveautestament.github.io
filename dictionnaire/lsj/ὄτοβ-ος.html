ὄτοβ-ος, ὁ, any loud noise, as the din of battle, ὄ. ἄπλητος Hes.Th.709; rattling of chariots, A.Th.151, 204 (both lyr.); crash of thunder, S.OC1479 (lyr.); also of the flute, γλυκὺν αὐλῶν ὄ. Id.Aj.1202 (lyr.): pl., ὄ. κροτάλων Antim.Eleg.Fr.17.—The freq. Mss. reading ὀττοβέω, ὄττοβος is disproved by the metre. (Onomatop.)
