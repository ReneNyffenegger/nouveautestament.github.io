ἀΐδηλος [ῐ], Dor. ἀΐδᾱλος, ον, (ἀ- priv., φιδεῖν) making unseen, annihilating, destructive: in Hom., as epith. of Arcs and Athena, Il.5.897,880; πῦρ ἀ. 2.455, al., Emp.109; ἠελίοιο ἔργ’ ἀΐδηλα Parm.10.3; ἀΐδαλος τύχα Epigr.Gr.240.5 (Smyrna); ἄτη Opp.H.2.487; πότμος ib.1.150. Adv. ἀϊδή-λως, = ὀλεθρίως, Il.21.220.     II. Pass., unseen , unknown, obscure, v.l. in Il.2.318, cf. Hes.Op.756, A.R.1.102, al.; unforeseen, ib.298; formless,4.681; unsubstantial, φρίκη Nic.Th.727; as epith. of Hades, dark, gloomy, S.Aj.608 (lyr.).
