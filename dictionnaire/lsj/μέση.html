μέση (sc. χορδή), ἡ, the mese, i. e. the top note of the lower tetrachord in the octave, originally the middle string of the seven-stringed (or of an earlier three-stringed) lyre, Pl.R.443d (cf. Sch.), Arist.Metaph.1018b29, Pr.919b20, Euc.Sect.Can.10; variously defined, Ptol.Harm.2.5, Cleonid.Harm.11, Bacch.Harm.65:—Dor. μέσσα Philol.6.     II. Geom., mean proportional, v. μέσος III. 5.     III. in punctuation (sc. στιγμή), dot placed midway between top and bottom of line, functioning as a comma, Satyr.6, D.T.630.6, Sch.D.T.p.177 H.
