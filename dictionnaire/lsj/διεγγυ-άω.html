Plu.Caes.11:—Pass., to be bailed by any one, ὀκτακοσίων ταλάντων τοῖς προξένοις διηγγυημένοι bailed by their Proxeni for eight hundred talents, Th.3.70.     2. give security, SIG976.49 (Samos, ii B. C.).     II. take pledges, distrain, ib.629.20 (ii B. C.).     III. abs., mortgage one's property, LXXNe.5.3.
