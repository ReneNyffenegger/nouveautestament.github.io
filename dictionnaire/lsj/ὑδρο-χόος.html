ὑδρο-χόος, ὁ, (χέω) water-pourer, name of the constellation Aquarius, Eudox. ap. Hipparch.1.2.20, Placit.1.6.6, Euc.Phaen.p.12 M., cf. AP12.199 (Strat.); contr. ὑδρο-χοῦς, Supp.Epigr.7.363.5, al. (Dura-Europus, ii A. D.):—dat. ὑδροχοῆϊ (as if from ὑδροχοεύς), Ep. for the common ὑδροχόῳ, Arat.389, Nonn.D.23.315.     II. name of an Egyptian month, = Φαρμοῦθι, POxy.465.11 (ii A. D.).
