ἐπικάρπ-ιος, ον, (καρπός) I) bringer or guardian of fruits, epith. of Zeus, Corn.ND9, Arist.Mu.401a19, Plu.2.1048c, etc.; of Hermes, IG 12(7).252 (Amorgos); θεοί Max.Tyr.30.4; fruit-bearing, ὧραι Arat.552.     2. τὸ ἐ. pedicle or fruit-stalk, Phanias ap.Ath.2.68c, cf. 51c.     II. (καρπός II) on or for the wrist, ἐ. ὄφεις bracelets in the shape of snakes, Philostr.Ep.22.     2. Subst. ἐπικάρπιον, τό, part of the hand near the wrist, Sor.1.84.
