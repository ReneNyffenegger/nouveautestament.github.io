δαῦκος, ὁ, an umbelliferous plant growing in Crete, Athamanta Cretensis, Hp.Acut.23, Dsc.3.72 (who applies the name to two other species, Peucedanum Cervaria and Psychotis Amnis), POxy.1088.65, Gal.6.654; also, = σταφυλῖνος, wild carrot, Daucus Carota, Id.11.862, which is called δαῦκον, τό, by Thphr.HP9.15.5 (but, = Malabaila aurea, ib.9.15.8,9.20.2): δαύκειον, τό, Nic.Th.858,939: δαυκίν (i.e. δαυκίον), Gp.12.1.2:—also δαυχμός, Nic.Th.94 (cf. Sch. ad loc.), Al.199.
