τρι-πάχυιος [ᾰ], ον, (παχύς) thrice-fattened, thrice- gorged, epith. of the δαίμων of the Atridae (perh. in allusion to the three visitations mentioned in A.Ch.1065-74), Ag.1476 (lyr.):—the word is doubtful; Bamberger proposed τριπάχυντον, referring to Th.771; Blomf. τριπάλαιον; Bamberger (olim) τριπαλαιστήν.
