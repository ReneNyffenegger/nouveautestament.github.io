λοιβ-ή, ἡ, (λείβω) pouring. only in religious sense, drink-offering, λοιβῇ τε κνίσῃ τε with drink- offering and burnt-offering, Il.9.500, cf. 4.49, 24.70; σοὶ δ’ αὖ λ. φέρον says Odysseus to the Cyclops, Od.9.349: later, like σπονδαί, χοαί (which are more freq.), in pl., Pi.N.11.6, S.El.52; λοιβαὶ Διός, offered to him, A.Fr.55. — Rare in Prose, λ. οἴνου Pl.Lg.906d, referring to Il.9.500.     2. of water generally, λ. Στυγός A.R.2.291.
