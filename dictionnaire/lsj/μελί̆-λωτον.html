μελί̆-λωτον, τό, Peripl.M.Rubr.49, and μελί̆-λωτος, ὁ, Sapph.Supp.25.14, Thphr.HP7.15.3:—melilot, Trigonella graeca, a kind of clover, so called from the quantity of honey it contained, μ. ἀνθεμώδης Sapph. l.c., cf.
