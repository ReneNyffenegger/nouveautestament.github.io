εὔδειπν-ος, ον, with goodly feasts, δαῖτες εὔ. well- appointed, sumptuous feasts, E.Med.200 (anap.).     II. epith. of departed souls to whom offerings were made (cf. foreg.), παρ’ εὐδείπνοις ἔσῃ ἄτιμος ἐμπύροισι κνισωτοῖς χθονός A.Ch.484; taken by some Gramm. as applied to the festival itself, Hsch., Phot.,EM42.3.
