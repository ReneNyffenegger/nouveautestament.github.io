ἰο-χέαιρα, ἡ, (ἰός A) arrow-pourer, shooter of arrows, epith. of Artemis, Il.5.53, etc.; ἰ. παρθένος Pi.P.2.9: as Subst., Ἰοχέαιρα Il.21.480, Od.11.198, Schwyzer 758 (vi B.C.), IG14.1389i53; later ἰ. φαρέτρα AP6.9 (Mnasalc.).     II. (ἰός B) poison-shedding, of serpents, Nic.Fr. 33. (-χέϝαιρα from χέω, not as expld. by Apollon.Lex. etc. from χαίρω.) [ῑ as in ἰός: yet ῐ in Pi.l.c.]
