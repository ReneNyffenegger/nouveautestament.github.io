ὄρημι, Aeol. for ὁράω, Sapph.2.11; 3sg. ὄρη Theoc.30.22, cf. Aeol. ἐπόρημι; part. ὄρεις Pittac. ap. D.L.1.81; to this athematic stem ὁρη- (not from ὁρα-) prob. belong 2sg. Med. ὅρηαι Od.14.343 and 2sg. Act. ποθόρησθα Theoc.6.8:—but many Dor. forms have η by contr. from ᾰε, as Lacon. inf. ὁρῆν Ar.Lys.1077, Syrac. imper. ὅρη Epich.170.12, etc.; v. ὁράω.
