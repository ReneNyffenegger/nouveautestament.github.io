ἀντίχειρ (sc. δάκτυλος, which is supplied in Heliod. ap. Orib.48.54.1, S.E.M.1.137, Gp.4.12.13), ὁ, thumb, as being opposite to the fingers, Sor.1.103, Dsc.5.79, Plu.2.761c, etc.     II. responsive to the touch, βόμβοι κυμβάλων Diog.Trag.1.4 (Casaubon).     III. Subst. (sc. σωλήν), inverse tube of alembic, Zos.Alch.p.225B.
