πληθ-ύς, ύος, ἡ, Ep. dat. πληθυῖ Il.22.458, Od.11.514, 16.105:—Ion. for πλῆθος, throng, crowd, of people, Hom.ll. cc.; = δῆμος, Leg.Gort. 6.52; majority, πληθὺν δὲ νικῆν IG9(1).333.18 (Locr., v B.C.): as Noun of multitude with pl. Verb, ὣς φάσαν ἡ π. Il.2.278: in later Prose, Pl.Ax.366b, LXX3 Ma.4.17; τῆς στρατιᾶς τὴν π. συχνήν Plu.Pomp.39, cf. Luc.Cont.15, etc.; = Lat. plebs, D.H.7.16, etc. [ῡ in nom. and acc. sg., in other cases ῠ.]
