ὀκρίβας [ῐ], αντος, ὁ, (ὄκρις, βαίνω) platform or tribune in the Odeum at Athens, on which the actors appeared at the Proagon, Pl.Smp.194b; but expld. as τὸ λογεῖον ἐφ’ οὗ οἱ τραγῳδοὶ ἠγωνίζοντο, Hsch.     2. = κόθορνος, Philostr.VA6.11, VS1.9.1: in pl., Id.VA5.9, Them.Or.26.316d, Luc.Ner.9.     II. generally, like κιλλίβας,     1. painter's easel, Poll.7.129.     2. raised seat of the chariot-driver, Hsch., Phot., Suid.     3. dub. sens., ὑπερθέντων ὀκρ[ίβαντα] IG12(8).261 (Thasos).     III. ass or wild goat, Anon. ap. Hsch.
