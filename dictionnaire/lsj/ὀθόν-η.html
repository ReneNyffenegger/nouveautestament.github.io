ὀθόν-η, ἡ, fine linen, in Hom. always pl., fine linen cloths, Od.7.107; of a woman's dress, ἀργεννῇσι καλυψαμένη ὀθόνῃσιν Il.3.141, cf. 18.595; ὀθόναις ἐσταλμένος Luc.DMort.3.2: sg., a cloth, Act.Ap.10.11, 11.5, Gal.11.134, 6.795.     2. later, sails, πνεύσεται εἰς ὀθόνας AP12.53.8 (Mel.), cf. 10.5 (Thyill.): sg., sail-cloth, sail, Luc.JTr.46, VH2.37.     3. in pl., of the membranes that enclose the pupil of the eye, Emp.84.8.
