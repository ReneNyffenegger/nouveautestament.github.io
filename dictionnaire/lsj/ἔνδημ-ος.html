Aeschin.1.45, Foed. ap. Th.5.47; endemic, νοσήματα Gal.15.429, 17(1).11; also τὰ ἔ. βιβλία applied to the surgical treatises of Hippocrates, Pall.in Hp.Fract.12.271C.
