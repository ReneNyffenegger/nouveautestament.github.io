Βρῐτόμαρτις, ἡ, acc. -ιν SIG527.29 (Dreros, iii B. C.; where B. is distd. fr. Artemis), name of Artemis in Crete, = virgo dulcis, acc. to Solin.11.8 (but a nymph in Call.Dian.190): gen. -εως, Str.10.4.14; -ιδος, EM214.23:—hence Βρῐτομάρτια, τά, festival at Delos, IG11 (2).145.34. (Derived from βρίτον, = ἀγαθόν, acc. to EM214.29.)
