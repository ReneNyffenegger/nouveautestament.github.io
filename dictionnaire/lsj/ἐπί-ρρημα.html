ἐπί-ρρημα, ατος, τό, that which is said afterwards:     I. in Old Comedy, a speech, commonly of trochaic tetrameters, spoken by the Coryphaeus after the Parabasis (as in Ar.Nu.575, Eq.565), Hsch., Suid.     II. adverb, D.H.Comp.2, etc.; περὶ ἐπιρρημάτων, title of work by Apollonius Dyscolus.     III. surname, nickname, Macho ap.Ath.13.578d.     IV. v. ἐπίρραμμα, ἐπίρριμμα.
