χρῖσμα ατος, τό, (χρίω) later form for χρῖμα (q.v.), found in codd. of X.Smp.2.4, An.4.4.13, Thphr.Od.8,15, Sor.1.4, Gal.6.402, etc.     II. anointing, unction, LXXEx.29.7, 35.15, Gal.10.892.     2. in NT of spiritual grace, χρῖσμα ἔχετε ἀπὸ τοῦ ἁγίου 1 Ep.Jo.2.20, cf. 27.     III. coating of wall, plaster, D.S.2.9, Luc.Hist.Conscr.62. (The usual accent χρίσμα is wrong, cf. χρῖμα.)
