ἀτάομαι [ᾱτ], Pass., (ἄτη) suffer, be in distress, in Trag. always in pres. part. ἀτώμενος S.Aj.384, Ant.17,314, E.Supp.182, exc. ἀτώμεσθα S.Aj.269; ἀτασθῶσιν is dub. in Hes.Cat.Oxy.1358 Fr. 2.13.     II. as law-term, αἴ τις ἀταθείη the injured party, Leg.Gort.4.29; but ὁ ἀταμένος the loser in a suit, ib.10.21; ἀϝατᾶται suffers a penalty, IG 5(1).1155 (Gythium):—Act., ἀτάω, aor. subj. 3 sg. ἀτάσῃ dub. in Leg.Gort.6.23,43.
