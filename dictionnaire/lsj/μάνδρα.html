μάνδρα, ἡ, enclosed space,     1. for cattle, fold, byre, Call.Cer.106, Theoc.4.61, Plu.2.648a; for horses, μάνδραις ἐν ἱππείαισιν S.Fr.659.3: in reference to the bezel in which the stone of a ring, with a design of oxen, is set, Pl.Epigr.21, AP9.746 (Polemo), cf. Hld.5.14.     2. dub. sens. in POxy.984 (i A. D.).     3. square on a draught-board, Mart.7.72.8 (pl.).
