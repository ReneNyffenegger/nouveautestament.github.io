ὀρει-τύπος [ῠ], ον, (τύπτω) working in the mountains: ὀρειτύποι, acc. to Gal.17(2).49, were wood-cutters and quarry-men, who brought down materials from the mountains :—so ὀρεοτύποι, Thphr.HP3.3.7, 3.12.4, al. (but ὀρει- CP5.11.3); ὀροιτύποι, Nic.Th.5,377, AP7.445 (Pers.), Eleg.Alex.Adesp.1.6; cf. also ὀροτύπος.
