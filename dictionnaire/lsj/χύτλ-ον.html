χύτλ-ον, τό, (χέω) anything that can be poured, liquid, fluid; esp.,     1. in pl., χύτλα, water for washing, the bath, Lyc.1099, Euph.9.7; but also, libations to the dead, Berl.Sitzb. 1927.161(Cyrene), A.R.1.1075, 2.927, Orph.A.32.     2. a mixture of water and oil, elsewh. ὑδρέλαιον, rubbed in after bathing, Erot. s.v. χυτλάζηται.     3. running water, stream, Lyc.701.
