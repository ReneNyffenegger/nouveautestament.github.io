στρόμβ-ος, ὁ, a body rounded or spun round: hence,     1. top, Il.14.413; ὥσπερ σ. στρέφεσθαι Luc.Asin.42.     2. = στροφάλιγξ, whirlwind, A.Pr.1084 (anap.), prob. in Id.Fr.195.3.     3. trumpet-shell, Arist.HA492a17, al.; sea-snail, Artem.2.14; of a shell used as a trumpet, conch, Lyc.250, Theoc.9.25, Plu.2.713b.     4. snail, Arist.HA548a18, cf. 530a6, PA661a23.     5. = στρόβιλος 6, Nic.Th.884.     6. spindle, Lyc.585.
