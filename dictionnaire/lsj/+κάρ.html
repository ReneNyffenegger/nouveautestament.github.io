*κάρ (A), a word of uncertain gender, nom. form, and meaning, typifying what is worthless, τίω δέ μιν ἐν κᾰρὸς αἴσῃ I value him at α . . 's worth, Il.9.378: καρός is variously expld. by Ar. Byz. etc. ap. Sch., e.g. as = κηρός or as pr.n. Καρός.     II. = κάρα, ἐπὶ κάρ head-long (nisi junctim scrib.), Il.16.392; ἀνὰ κάρ upwards, Hp. ap. Gal.19.79.
