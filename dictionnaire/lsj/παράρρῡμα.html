παράρρῡμα or παράρῡμα, ατος, τό, (ἐρύω A) anything drawn along or over something:     1. leathern or hair curtain, stretched along the sides of ships to protect the men, X.HG1.6.19, IG22.1629.451, 1668.86, al., Moschio ap.Ath.5.208c (Casaubon for παρατρήματα), LXXEx.35.11.     2. π. ποδός covering for the foot, S.Fr.527.     3. pl., of fasteners for bandages, Gal.18(2).748 (nisi leg. παραρραμμάτων).
