ὑδέω [ῠ], (ὕδης) call, name, Call.Fr.anon.62, Nic.Al.47,525; Ep. also ὑδείω, Call.Jov.76:—Pass., to be told of, to be called so and so, Arat.257, A.R.2.528, 4.264:—Suid. and Et.Gud.539.56 also quote the form ὕδειν (from ὕδω), and Theognost.Can.19 has ὕδειν· τρέχειν, λέγειν:—ὑδεῖν should perh. be restored for ἰδεῖν in E.Hyps.iii 15, where it would mean tell of, celebrate; [ὑ]δέοντος is suggested in PLit.Lond.60.9 (Posidipp.).
