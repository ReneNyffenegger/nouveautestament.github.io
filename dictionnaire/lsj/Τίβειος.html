Τίβειος [ῐ], name of a slave, IG22.1672.69, 2937.7, 2940.8, Men.1075, Her.21, al., prob. cj. in Thphr.Char.9.3; later Τίβιος [–⏑⏑] AP14.123.11 (Metrod., Τίμιος cod.): originally a Paphlagonian name, Str.7.3.12, 12.3.25, who implies that it was not the name of a people; cf. however Τίβειον ὄρος (v.l. τόπος) in Phrygia, St.Byz.; Τίβιοι καὶ Φρύγες Gal.10.4; Τιβία, = ὅλη ἡ Φρυγία, App.Prov.3.79.
