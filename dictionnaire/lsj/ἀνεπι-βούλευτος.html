Pass., not plotted against, not liable to attack, ἀ. φθόνῳ Com.Adesp.1212, cf. Plb.7.8.4, Agatharch.42, Ael.NA9.59, etc. Adv. ἀνεπιβουλεύ-τως Ph.2.645, Suid. s.v. Ξάνθος.
