Ἀργώ, όος, contr. οῦς, ἡ, (ἀργός, ή, όν) Argo, the ship in which Jason sailed to Colchis, the Swift, first in Od.12.70:—Adj. Ἀργῷος, α, ον, of the Argo, δόρυ, σκάφος, E.Andr.793 (lyr.), Med.477.     2. the constellation Argo, Eudox. ap. Hipparch.1.2.20, Arat.342, etc.     3. tree of whose timber the Argo was built, Hsch.
