Ἑλλήσ-ποντος, ὁ, Hellespont or sea of Helle (daughter of Athamas, who was drowned therein), now the Dardanelles, Il.2.845, Hdt.4.38, etc.:—sts. taken to include the Propontis, Id.1.57, etc.:—the adjacent country, Th.2.9, etc.: in this sense without Art. in Att. Inscrr., IG12.106.16, al.: said to be used of the Aegean, Str.7 Fr. 58.
