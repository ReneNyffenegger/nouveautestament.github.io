Gem.16.11, Cleom.1.4, Ptol.Alm.2.6; π. τοῦ ὁρίζοντος the zenith, Euc.Phaen.p.18 M.; λόξωσις τοῦ π. inclination of the pole (of the zodiac), Ocell.2.23.     b. pole-star, Eratosth.Cat.2.     3. celestial sphere, vault of heauen, sky, A.Pr.429 (lyr.), E.Fr.839.11 (anap.); ἄστρων π.
