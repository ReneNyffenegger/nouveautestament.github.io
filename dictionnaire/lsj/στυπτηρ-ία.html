στυπτηρ-ία, Ion. στυπτηρ-ίη (sc. γῆ), ἡ, name of any of a group of astringent substances containing (a) alum or (b) ferrous sulphate (χαλκῖτις (q.v.)), Hdt.2.180, freq. in Hp. (e.g. Ulc.14), Arist.HA547a20, Mir.842b22, PCair.Zen.326 bis 26 (iii B.C.), Ti.Locr.99d, Sor.1.50, Aret.CA1.9, POxy.1429.4 (iii/iv A.D.), PHolm.1.4,7, al.     II. in Egypt, the alum monopoly, POxy.2116 (iii A.D.).
