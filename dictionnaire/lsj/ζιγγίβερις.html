ζιγγίβερις, εως, Edict.Diocl.32.68 (Delph., Clit.), ὁ, ἡ (both in Edict.Diocl. l.c.); or ζιγγίβερι, τό, Dsc. (codd. opt.) and Gal. ll. cc.:—an Arabian spice-plant, the root of which was used in medicine, prob. ginger, Dsc.2.160, Gal.6.572, 11.880. (Perh. fr. Malay inchi-ver (inchi = root) through Skt. śr̥ṅgaveram and a Prakrit singaber.)
