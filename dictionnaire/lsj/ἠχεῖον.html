ἠχεῖον, τό, (ἦχος) drum, gong, Plu.Crass.23, Apollod. ap. Sch.Theoc.2.36, Procop.Gaz.Ecphr.p.153B.; tambourine, as head-dress, Herm.Trism. in Rev.Phil.32.254; used for stage-thunder, Sch.Ar.Nu.292; as sounding-boards in the theatre, Vitr.5.5.2.     II. in the lyre, = χάλκωμα, apptly. a metallic sounding-plate, Hsch.; so of the palate, Gal.UP7.5.     2. Adj. ἠχεῖος, ἠχεῖον ὄργανον sounding instrument, Ph.1.588, cj. ib.444, 510.
