ἀποδακρ-ύω [ῡ], weep much for, lament loudly, τινά Pl.Phd.116d; τι Plu.Sull.12.     2. ἀ. γνώμην weep away one's judgement, be melted to tears contrary to it, Ar.V.983.     3. to be made to weep by the use of collyrium, and so have the eyes purged, Arist.Pr.958b6, Luc.Peregr.45, Gal.12.751.     4. of trees, weep, drip gum, etc., ἀ. ῥητίνην Plu.2.640d.     II. cease to weep, ἀπολοφυράμενοι καὶ ἀποδακρύσαντες Aristox.Fr.Hist.90, cf.
