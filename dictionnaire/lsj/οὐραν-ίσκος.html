οὐραν-ίσκος, ὁ, Dim. of οὐρανός, a little heaven or sky: hence,     I. vaulted ceiling, esp. top of a tent, canopy, Callix.2, Phylarch.41 J., Plu.Alex.37, Phoc.33.     II. roof of the mouth, Sor.2.62, Gal.UP11.10, Ath.7.315d, v.l. in Arist.Pr.963a2.     III. a constellation of the southern hemisphere, Corona Australis, Sch.Arat.400.
