ἄθεος (A), ον, without God, denying the gods, esp. those recognized by the state, Pl.Ap.26c, etc.: applied to Diagoras, Cic.ND1.23.63; παράδειγμα ἄ., opp. θεῖον, Pl.Tht.176e.     2. generally, godless, ungodly, Pi.P.4.162, A.Eu.151, S.Tr.1036: Comp. -ώτερος Lys.6.32: Sup. -ώτατος X.An.2.5.39.     3. abandoned of the gods, S.OT661; μανίαι B.10.109.     4. not derived from the name of a god, ὀνόματα Clearch.63.     II. Adv. ἀθέ-ως by the anger of heaven (cf. I.3), ἀ. ἐφθαρμένη S.OT254, cf.El.1181: Sup. -ώτατα in most unholy wise, ib.124 (lyr.).
