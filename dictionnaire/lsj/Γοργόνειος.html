Γοργόνειος, ον, = Γοργεῖος, πεδία A.Pr.793; τὸ Γ. a Gorgon-mask, IG2.654,al., cf. Plu.Them.10; the Gorgon's head, a group of stars in Perseus, Gem.3.11, Ptol.Alm.7.5, etc., Plu.Them.10; but also, the constellation Medusa, Hipparch.2.3.27, al.; bogey, Ruf. ap. Orib.inc.20.27; Orphic name for the moon, because of the face in it, Orph.Fr.33.     II. Γοργόνειον, τό, = λιθόσπερμον, Ps.-Dsc.3.141.
