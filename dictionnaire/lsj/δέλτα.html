δέλτα, τό, indecl., the letter δέλτα: gen. δέλτατος Democr.20. (Hebr. dāleth 'door'.)     II. anything shaped like a Δ, esp. island formed by the mouths of a large river, as the Nile, Hdt.2.13, etc.; of the Indus, Str.15.1.33, Arr.An.5.4.1, etc.     2. adverbially, δ. παρατετιλμέναι Ar.Lys.151.     III. = δελτωτόν, Ptol.Tetr.27.
