χᾰμαί-πῐτυς, ῠος, ἡ, ground-pine, Ajuga Chamaepitys, Nic.Al.56, Apollod. ap. Ath.15.681d, Dsc.3.158, Plin.HN24.29.     2. mountain germander, Teucrium montanum, Dsc. l. c., Plin. l.c.     3. herb-ivy, Ajuga Iva, Dsc. and Plin. ll.cc.     4. = ὑπερικόν, Dsc.3.154.     5. = μυρσίνη ἀγρία, Ps.-Dsc.4.144:—hence χᾰμαιπῐτύϊνος οἶνος wine flavoured with one of these plants, Dsc.5.70.
