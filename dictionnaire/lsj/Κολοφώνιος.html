Κολοφώνιος, α, ον, of or from Colophon in Ionia, Hdt.1.147, etc.; Κ. σχῆμα, a figure of speech, such as using ἡ κεφαλὴ τῷ ἀνθρώπῳ for τοῦ -που, Lesb.Gramm.7: Subst., ἡ Κολοφωνία (sc. ῥητίνη), Colophonian gum, resin, Dsc.1.71, Gal.13.475, Hippiatr.20, al., PGrenf. 1.52.7 (iii A.D.); also, = σκαμμωνία, Ps.-Dsc.4.170; Κολοφώνιον (sc. ὑπόδημα), τό, kind of shoe, Rhinth.4; also, a measure used in Egypt, Ostr.1166, 1265 (ii A.D.); but also, a kind of vegetable, PTeb.419.21 (iii A.D.).
