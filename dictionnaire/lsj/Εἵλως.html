Εἵλως, ωτος, Th.4.80, etc., and Εἱλώτης, ου, ὁ, Hdt.6.58, etc.; fem. Εἱλωτίς, ίδος, ἡ, Plu.Ages.3:—Helot, name of the Spartan serfs, derived by Hellanic.188 J., Theopomp.Hist.14, etc., from Ἕλος, a town of Laconia, whose inhabitants were enslaved: by others from Pass. of *ἕλω, = αἱρέω, cf. EM332.53.
