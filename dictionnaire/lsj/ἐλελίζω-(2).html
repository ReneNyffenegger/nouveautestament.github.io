ἐλελίζω (B), aor. ἠλέλιξα X.An.5.2.14, Ep. ἐλ- Call.Del.137:— cry ἐλελεῦ, hence, raise the battle- cry, τῷ Ἐνυαλίῳ X.An.1.8.18: generally, raise a loud cry, E.Ph.1514 (lyr.); of a shield, ring, Call. l.c.:—Med., of the nightingale, trill her lay of sorrow, E.Hel.1111 (lyr.): c. acc., Ἴτυν ἐλελιζομένη trilling her lament for Itys, Ar.Av.213 (lyr., but punctuation is dub.).
