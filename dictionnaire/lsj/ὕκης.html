ὕκης [ῡ], ὁ, a sea-fish, Antim. ap. Ath.7.304e (om. Kinkel), Philet.20, Call.Frr.72, 156; also as fem. (perh. fr. nom. ὕκη), ὕκας ἀγεληΐδας Numen. ap. Ath.7.320d, 327b; also ὕκος, or ὗκος, ὁ, Hsch.: said by Zenod. ap. Ath.7.327c to be Cyren. for ἐρυθρῖνος, by Hermipp.Hist.ibid.(=Fr.74) to be = ἰουλίς.
