σίλλῠβος, ὁ, parchment-label, appended to the outside of a book, Cic.Att.4.4a.1, 4.8.2, dub. in 4.5.3 (v. infr.): pl. σίλλυβα, τά, = θύσανοι, Poll.7.64. (In Cic.Att.4.5.3 the vv.ll. sittybis and sic tu iubes are perh. traces of sittybis; but σιττύβαι (q.v.) has an inappropriate meaning, unless it can mean leather case of a book.)
