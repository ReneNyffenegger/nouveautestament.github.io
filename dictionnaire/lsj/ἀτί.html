(τῑμή II) not valued or estimated, Is.3.35: esp. δίκη ἀ. a cause in which the penalty is not assessed in court, but fixed by law beforehand, D.21.90, Aeschin.3.210; opp. τιμητός (where the penalty is settled in court), D.27.67, cf. Poll.8.54,63, Harp. s.v.; Suid. erroneously reverses this explanation.     2. invaluable, priceless, LXXWi.7.9, Eust.781.19.     3. not assessed, IG5(1).1433.45 (Messene); not capable of being valued, ἄγαλμα Epigr.Gr.805.
