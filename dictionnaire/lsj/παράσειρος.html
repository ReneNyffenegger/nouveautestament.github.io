παράσειρος, ον, (σειρά) tied or fastened alongside, π. ἵππος a horse harnessed alongside of the regular pair, = σειραφόρος, Poll.1.141, Them.Or.4.50a: metaph., associate, E.Or.1017 (lyr.).     II. generally, at the side, X.Cyn.5.23, Ael.NA15.10; παράσειρα, τά, parts on each side of the tongue, Ruf.Onom.57, Poll.2.107, cf. περισείρια; δύο [πλευραὶ] π. two lowest of the true ribs, ib.182.
