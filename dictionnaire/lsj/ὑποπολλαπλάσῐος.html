ὑποπολλαπλάσῐος [πλᾰ], ον, of a number, submultiple of another, Nicom.Ar.1.17,18,19:—also ὑποπολλαπλᾰσῐ-επιμόριος, ον, submultiple- superparticular, i.e. contained in another number several times with one fractional part remaining:—and ὑποπολλαπλᾰσῐ-επιμερής, ές, submultiple-superpartient, i.e. contained in another several times with two or more fractional parts remaining, ib.1.17,21 (cf. 23).
