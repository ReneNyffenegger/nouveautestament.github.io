Ὕης [ῡ], ου, ὁ, (ὕω) epith. of Ζεὺς ὄμβριος (cf. ὑέτιος), Hsch., Theognost.Can.18.     II. epith. of Dionysus, Cli<to>dem.21, Euph.14 (in Hsch. also Ὑεύς, in Sch.Ar.Av.874 Ὕας), as the god of fertilizing moisture, Plu.2.364d: cf. Ὑάδες II, Ὕη. The invocation Ὕης ἄττης in D.18.260 is of doubtful meaning; Ὕης is placed among ξενικοὶ θεοί by Ar.Fr.878 (cf. Apolloph.7). (Acc. to Hdn.Gr.1.59 Ὑῆς is the correct accent.)
