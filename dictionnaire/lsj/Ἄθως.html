Ἄθως [ᾰ], ω, ὁ, acc. Ἄθω Aeschin.3.132, Theoc.7.77, etc., but in earlier writers Ἄθων, Hdt.6.44, 7.22, Th.5.3: Ep. gen. Ἀθόω Il.14.229; later gen. Ἄθωος Str.Fr.33:—mount Athos, Ἄθως σκιάζει νῶτα Λημνίας βοός (prov. of those whose influence is felt at a distance, from the shadow cast by Athos) S.Fr.776.
