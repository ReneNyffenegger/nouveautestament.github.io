ἑτερο-μήκης, ες, with sides of uneven length, i.e. oblong, X.Eq.7.14; ἑτερόμηκες, τό, oblong rectangle, Arist.Cat.11a10, de An.413a17, Euc.1 Def.22.     2. of numbers, not square, i.e. produced by the multiplication of two unequal factors, as 6 = 3 x 2, Pl.Tht.148a, Plu.2.367f; opp. ἰσόπλευρος (both of line and number), Arist.APo.73b1.
