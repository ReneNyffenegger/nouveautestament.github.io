χαλκό-κροτος, ον, sounding or rattling with bronze, epith. of Demeter, in allusion to instruments used in her worship, Pi.I.7(6).3; χ. ἵπποι horses that stamp with hoofs of bronze, brazen-hoofed, Ar.Eq.552 (lyr.).     II. = χαλκήλατος, φάσγανον E.Ph.1577 (lyr.); χαλκόκροτα bronze utensils, POxy.1657.1 (iii A. D.).     III. χαλκοκρότος, ὁ, = Lat. aerarius, Gloss. (καλκόκροτος cod.).
