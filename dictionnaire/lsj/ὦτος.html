ὦτος, ου, ὁ (on the accent v. Hdn.Gr.1.214), a horned or eared owl, ὁ δ’ ὦτος . . , περὶ τὰ ὦτα πτερύγια ἔχων Arist.HA597b21, cf. Plu.2.961e, v.l. ib.52b: Ath.9.390d appears to identify it with the ὠτίς, but this is due to interpolation.     II. an easily deceived person, booby, Com.Adesp.47, Ael.Dion.Fr.336.     III. ὦτα· τὰ μὴ στρογγύλα, Hsch.; but ὦτοι λίθοι in IG4.823.66 (Troezen) is wrongly read, v. ὧ II.
