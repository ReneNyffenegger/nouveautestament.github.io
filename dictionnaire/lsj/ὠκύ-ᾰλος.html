ὠκύ-ᾰλος [ῠ], ον, perh. sea-swift, speeding o'er the sea, epith. of a ship, Il.15.705, Od.12.182, 15.473, S.Aj.710 (lyr.), Mosch.2.60.     2. later, generally, swift, violent, ῥιπή Opp.H.2.535, cf. Pi.Parth.2.19. (It is doubtful whether -αλος comes from ἅλς; see opinions of D.H. and Hdn.Gr. ap. Sch.Il. l.c.)
