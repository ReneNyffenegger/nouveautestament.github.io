σύ̆αγρ-ος, ὁ, name of a dog, S.Fr.154.     II. = σῦς ἄγριος or ἀγρία, wild boar or sow, wild swine, Antiph.42, Dionys.Trag.1.2, PRyl.238.3 (iii A.D.), etc.; σ. ἄρρην Gal.12.633; un-Attic acc. to Phryn.358.     III. name of a kind of frankincense, Dsc.1.68 codd. (Συάγριος cj. Wellmann, i.e. obtained from Σύαγρος in Arabia).     2. a kind of date, Plin.HN13.42.
