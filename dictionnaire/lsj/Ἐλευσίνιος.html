Ἐλευσίνια, τά, their festivals, ib.12.5, 22.847.24, Hyp.Fr.112, Paus.4.33.5, etc.: prov., Ἀττικοὶ τὰ Ἐ., of groups of persons confabulating, Duris95 J.: Lacon. Ἐλευhύνια, τά, IG5(1).213.11 (v B.C.).     IV. Ἐλευσίνιος, ὁ (sc. μήν), name of month in Crete, GDI5183: also spelt Ἐλευσύνιος SIG712.8 (Olus), and so in Thera, Test.Epict.2.7,3.3.
