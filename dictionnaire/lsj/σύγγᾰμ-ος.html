σύγγᾰμ-ος, ον, united in wedlock, married, ἄλλῳ to another, E.El.212 (lyr.); σ. δάμαρ Lyc.1220: as Subst. σύγγαμος, ὁ or ἡ, husband or wife, Orph.A.595, IG 12(3).1188 (Melos).     2. generally, connected by marriage, E.Ph.[428].     3. σύγγαμός σοι Ζεύς sharing your marriage-bed, of Amphitryon, Id.HF149: in pl., the rival wives of one man, Id.Andr.182.
