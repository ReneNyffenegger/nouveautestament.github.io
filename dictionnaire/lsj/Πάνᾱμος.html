Πάνᾱμος [πᾰ], ὁ, name of a month, at Argos, Mnemos.44.51; at Epidaurus, IG(1).117.9, al.; at Megara, ib.7.188; at Thespiae, ib.1720:—also written Πάνεμος (Boeot.), Plu.Cam.19; Πάνημος, D.18.157 (Corinth.), Call.Epigr.46, IG11(2).203 A31 (Delos, iii B. C.), PCair.Zen.20.5 (iii B. C.):—hence Πανάμια, τά, festival at Thespiae, Schwyzer 491.11.
