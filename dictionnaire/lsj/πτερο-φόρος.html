πτερο-φόρος, ον, feathered, winged, δέμας A.Ag.1147 (lyr.); θεαί E.Or.317 (lyr.); φῦλα the feathered tribes, Ar.Av.1757 (lyr.): metaph., π. Διὸς βέλος the winged bolt of Zeus, ib.1714.     II. under the Roman Emperors, as Subst., courier, Plu.Oth.4; cf. πτεροφόρας II.     III. πτεροφόρου, gen. sg. of this or the foreg. word, dub. sens. in PCair.Zen. 512 (iii B.C.).
