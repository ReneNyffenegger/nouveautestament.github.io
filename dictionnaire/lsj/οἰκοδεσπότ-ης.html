οἰκοδεσπότ-ης, ου, ὁ, master or steward of a house, Alex.225, Ev.Matt.10.25, PMeyer 24.2 (vi A. D.): metaph., of God, Arr.Epict.3.22.4 (οἰκίας δ. was preferred by the Atticists, as in Pl.Lg.954b: so οἴκων δεσπόται X.Mem.2.1.32, cf. Phryn.348).     2. native ruler, opp. foreign emperor, J.Ap.2.11.     II. Astrol., of a planet, owner of a domicile or otherwise predominant, Ptol.Tetr.97, Porph. ap. Iamb.Myst.9.5, Heph. Astr.1.13, PSI3.158.80 (iii A. D.).
