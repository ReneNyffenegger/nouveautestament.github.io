X.Mem.2.1.5, Arist.EN1129b21.     III. metaph., in fut. Med. (in pass. sense), οὐ μοιχεύσεταί μου τὰ φιλήματα her kisses shall not be adulterously stolen from me, Ach.Tat.4.8.
