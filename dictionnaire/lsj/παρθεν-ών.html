παρθεν-ών, ῶνος, ὁ, maidens' apartments in a house, mostly in pl., A.Pr.646, E.Ph.89, IT826, etc.     II. in sg., the western cella of the Parthenon or temple of Athena at Athens, IG12.301.13, al., D.22.76, etc.; also, of the cella of the temple of Artemis at Magnesia on Maeander, SIG 695.23 (ii B. C.); of the Great Mother at Cyzicus, Michel 538.6, and Hermione, IG4.743.     III. name of a month at Alexandria, Ptol.Alm.11.3.
