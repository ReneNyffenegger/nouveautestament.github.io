Χάρυβδις [ᾰ], εως, Ion. ιος, ἡ, Charybdis, a whirlpool on the coast of Sicily, opposite Scylla, Od.12.104, E.Tr.436, Th.4.24, Str.6.2.3.     2. generally, whirlpool, gulf, πάντα μίαν ἱκνεῖται χ. Simon.38, cf. E.Supp.500, Str.6.2.9.     3. metaph. of a rapacious person, χ. ἁρπαγῆς Ar.Eq.248 (troch.).
