ἀμφιμέλας, -μέλαινα, -μέλᾰν, black all round: Hom. always epith. of φρένες (best written divisim, as by Alex. critics), darkened on either side, of strong emotions, as anger, Il.1.103, 17.83, Od.4.661; courage, Il.17.499,573: prob. metaph. from an angry sea.     2. generally, ἀ. κόνις coal-black dust, AP7.738 (Theodorid.).
