Choerob.in Theod.1.340, Sch.D.T. l.c.:—κατὰ στοιχεῖον in the order of the letters, alphabetically, AP11.15 (Ammian.); dub.sens.in Plu.2.422e.     2. in Physics, στοιχεῖα were the components into which matter is ultimately divisible, elements, reduced to four by Empedocles, who called them ῥιζώματα, the word στοιχεῖα being first used (acc. to Eudem. ap.
