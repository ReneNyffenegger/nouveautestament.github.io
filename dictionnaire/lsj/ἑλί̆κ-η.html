ἑλί̆κ-η, ἡ, (ἕλιξ) winding: hence,     I. the constellation of the Great Bear, from its revolving round the pole, Arat.37, A.R.3.1195.     II. convolution of a spiral shell, Arist.HA524b12, PA680a22,al.; of the bowels, ib.682a15; of the ear, Id.GA781b15.     III. in Arcadia, crack willow, Salix fragilis, from its pliant nature, Thphr.HP3.13.7.
