ἀρουρ-αῖος [ᾰρ], α, ον, of or from the country, rural, rustic, μῦς ἀ. field-vole, Hdt.2.141; σμίνθος A.Fr.227; ὦ παῖ τῆς ἀ. θεοῦ, of Euripides as the reputed son of a herb-seller, Ar.Ra.840; ἀ. Οἰνόμαος, of Aeschines, who played the part of Oenomaüs 'in the provinces', D.18.242, cf. AB211 sq.; ἀ. λίθοι rough stones, SIG2587.21; φυτὰ ἀ. field-weeds, Thphr.HP7.6.1.
