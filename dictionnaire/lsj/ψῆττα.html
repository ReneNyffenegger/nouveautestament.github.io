ψῆττα, ἡ, a kind of flat-fish, prob. turbot, Rhombus maximus, Ar.Lys.115,131, Pl.Smp.191d, Antiph.132.7 (anap.), Ath.7.329e, Luc.Pisc.49, Alciphr.1.7; ψ. χονδροφυής perh. a skate, MatroConv.27.     II. a nickname for a glutton, Pl.Com.106. (The form ψῆσσα Alex. Trall.1.15, al., Zonar.; ψησία (s.v.l.) Suid.)
