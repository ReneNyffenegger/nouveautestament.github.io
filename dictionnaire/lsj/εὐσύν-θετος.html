εὐσύν-θετος, ον, easy to compound into a word, λόγος Arist.Rh.1406a36. Adv. εὐσυνθέ-τως suitably, ἐκλέγεσθαι Eust.2.22; prob. in PLit.Lond.170 (i A.D.).     2. easy to put together or construct, Ph.Bel.56.34, Apollod.Poliorc.155.16.     II. of persons, in act. sense, constructive, inventive, εἰς τὸ νοῆσαι Man.5.272.     2. easy to deal with, Ptol.Tetr.165.
