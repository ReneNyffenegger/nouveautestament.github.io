Ἀμφικτύον-ες, ων, οἱ, Amphictyons, deputies of states associated in an ἀμφικτυονία, e.g. at Onchestos, Str.9.2.33; Calauria, 8.6.14; Delos, Ath.6.234e; esp. at Delphi, D.5.14,al., Aeschin.2.115, etc.; Ἀμφικτίονες IG2.545.16,al., and this form seems to preserve the etym., v. foreg.: sg. in Lib.Decl.17.10,al.
