κῠν-άγχη, ἡ, (κύων, ἄγχω) dog-quinsy, Arist.HA604a5, Ant.Lib.23.2; cf. ὑάγχη: hence,     II. sore throat, Hp.VM19, Prog.23, Aph.3.16 (all pl.), Porph.Abst.3.7: συνάγχη is a constant v.l., but Gal. distinguishes κυνάγχη as an inflammation of the larynx, συνάγχη of the interior muscles of the throat, παρασυνάγχη of the exterior muscles, 8.248, 17(2).706.     III. dog's collar, AP6.34 (Rhian., v.l. κυνακτάν), 35 (Leon.).     IV. pillory, Hsch.
