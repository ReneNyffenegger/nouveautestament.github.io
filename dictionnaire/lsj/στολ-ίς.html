στολ-ίς, ίδος, ἡ, = στολή II, garment, robe, E.Ph.1491 (lyr.), AP7.27 (Antip. Sid.), Sammelb.6178 (Egypt, metr.), etc.; νεβρῶν στολίδες, i.e. fawnskins worn as garments, E.Hel.1359 (lyr.).     2. sails, AP10.6 (Satyr.).     II. pl., folds in a woman's robe, πέπλων E.Ba.936; τῶν ἀνδριάντων Arist.Aud.802a38; cf. στολιδωτός: of wrinkles or folds in the womb or other parts, Sor.1.14, Heliod. ap. Orib.44.8.14; of rugose ulcers, Gal.12.231, al.; also wrinkles on the forehead, Poll.2.46, cf. Plu.2.64a.
