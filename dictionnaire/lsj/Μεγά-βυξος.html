Μεγά-βυξος, ὁ, a Persian pr. n. (Hdt.3.70, al.) and later title of generals and priests, Bagabukhša, lit. 'set free by God', Hdt.3.153, al., SIG 282.2 (Priene, iv B.C.), X.An.5.3.6, Str.14.1.23, App.BC5.9, Hsch., etc.:—hence μεγα-βύξειοι λόγοι, boastings, Id. (Usu. Μεγά-βυζ-ος in codd., as Str., App., and Hsch. ll.cc., but Μεγά-βυξ-ος in SIG l.c., v.l. in Hdt.3.153, 160, al., -bux- Quint.5.12.21 codd., -byx- Plin.HN35.93 codd. opt.)
