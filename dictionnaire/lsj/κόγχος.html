Dsc.1.30.     II. anything like a mussel-shell,     1. upper part of the skull, Lyc.1105.     2. boss of a shield, Plb.l.c.     3. small iron crucible, Dsc.5.95.     4. socket of the eye, Poll.2.71 (pl.).     5. kneepan, ib.188.     III. soup of lentils boiled with the pods, Timo3.
