αἰγιᾰλός, ὁ, sea-shore, beach, Il.4.422, Od.22.385, Hdt.7.50, al., Th.1.7 (pl.), X.An.6.4.4, Thphr.HP7.13.8 (pl.), etc.; distinguished from ἀκτή, Arist.HA547a10; also in E.(lyr.), IT425, IA210; αἰγιαλὸν ἔνδον τρέφει a whole beach of voting-pebbles, Ar.V.110: prov., αἰγιαλῷ λαλεῖς, of deaf persons, Suid., Zen.1.38.
