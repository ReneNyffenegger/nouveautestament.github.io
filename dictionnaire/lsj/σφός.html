σφός, σφή, σφόν, their, their own, belonging to them, Il.1.534, Sapph.10, Pi.P.5.102, etc. (never in Att.).     2. in post-Hom. Poets also, his or her, his own or her own, Hes.Th.398, Alcm.56A, Thgn.712, Call.Aet.Oxy.2080.75.     II. = σός, Orph.L.168.     2. = ἐμός, CR11.136 (Phrygia, metr.).     3. = σφωΐτερος, your (in addressing a pair), v.l. in Il.11.142. (σφός is to σφε, σφέτερος as *ἡμός (ἁμός) to ἁμέ, ἡμέτερος.)
