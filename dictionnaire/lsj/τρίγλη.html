τρίγλη, ἡ, red mullet, Mullus barbatus, Epich.64, Sophr.50, Cratin.58,320, Philyll.13, Diocl.Fr.135, PCair.Zen.83.2 (iii B. C.), Sor.1.51, 94, Gal.6.715; τ. μιλτοπάρηος MatroConv.27:—in later writers τρίγλᾰ or τρῖγλα prevailed, and is sts. found in codd. of earlier authors, as Arist.HA543a5, 591b19; τρῖγλαν (v.l. τρίγλαν) ἀπ’ ἀνθρακιῆς AP6.105 (Apollonid.); τρίγλαν Corn.ND34, Plu.2.730b, 977f, 983f; but only τρίγλη is recognized by Hdn.Gr.1.255 note, 1.318, Ath.7.324c.
