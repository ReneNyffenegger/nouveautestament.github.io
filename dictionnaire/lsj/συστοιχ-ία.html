συστοιχ-ία, ἡ, column or series of things or ideas, Arist.APr.66b27, Metaph.1004b27, 1066a15, 1072a31, Thphr.CP6.5.6; ἐκ τῶν σ. ὅσαι μὴ ἐπαλλάττουσιν ἀλλήλαις from series which are mutually exclusive, Arist.APo.79b7; ἐν τῇ αὐτῇ σ. τῆς κατηγορίας in the same line of predication, Id.Metaph.1054b35, 1058a13; esp. in Pythag. philosophy, pair of co- ordinate or parallel columns, αἱ ἀρχαὶ αἱ κατὰ συστοιχίαν λεγόμεναι in a series of co-ordinate pairs, as odd and even, one and many, right and left, ib.986a23; also, either of such parallel columns, ib.1093b12, PA670b21, EN1096b6, al., Thphr.Vent.58, Gal.18(2).167.
