προφορ-έομαι, Med., in weaving, carry on the web by passing the weft to and fro across the warp (the setting up of which is διάζεσθαι), Poll.7.32, Hsch., Phot., Suid., prob. in Call.Fr.244: hence, of a spider, τὴν ὁδὸν προφορεῖσθαι run to and fro, Call.Com.2, cf. Ar.Av.4; of dogs, π. παρὰ τὰ αὐτά X.Cyn.6.15.
