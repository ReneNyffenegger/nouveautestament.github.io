σάρον [ᾰ], τό, (σαίρω (B)) broom, besom, IG42(1).122.48 (Epid., iv B.C.), Pythagorei ap.Plu.2.727c, AP11.207 (Lucill.).     II. sweepings, refuse, rubbish, τὸ σ. ἄνελε Sophr.160, cf. Thphr.Metaph.15 (prob. cj.); of sea-weed, Call.Del.225: Com., of an old woman, παλαιὸν οἰκίας σάρον Ion Trag.9.—The Atticists (Phryn.63) rejected the word, but cf. Poll.6.94, 10.29.     III. σ. σιδαροῦν dub. in Supp.Epigr.6.171 (Acmonia).
