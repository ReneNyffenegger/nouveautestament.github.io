Ψ ψ, ψεῖ (q. v.), τό, indecl., twenty-third letter of the Ionic alphabet, Pl.Cra.427a, Callias ap.Ath.10.453d: not used in the Attic alphabet: in the Western Gr. alphabets it represents the sound χ (kh): with the value ps it is found in SIG1 (Nubia, vi B. C.), etc.: as a numeral, ψʹ = 700, but ͵ψ = 700,000.
