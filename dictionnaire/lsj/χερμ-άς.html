χερμ-άς, άδος, ἡ, large pebble or stone, esp. for throwing or slinging, sling-stone, τηλεβόλος Pi.P.3.49; ὀκριόεσσα A.Th.300 (lyr.); κραταίβολος E.Ba.1096: of pebbles on the seabeach, A.R.2.695 (cf. στία), AP7.693 (Apollonid.); also in later Prose, D.H.9.21, al.     II. in later Poets, large block of stone, Lyc.20, 616, AP7.371 (Crin.).
