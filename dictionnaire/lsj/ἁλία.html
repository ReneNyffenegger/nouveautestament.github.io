D.18.90; at Corcyra, IG9(1).682; in Sicily and Magna Graecia, IG14.952 (Agrigentum), 612 (Rhegium), Tab.Heracl.1.118, 2.10; at Epidamnus and Tarentum, Arist.Pol.1301b23 (prob. cj. for ἡλιαία).     II. generally, meeting, assembly, ἁλίην ποιεῖσθαι Hdt.5.29,79; of the Persians, 1.125.
