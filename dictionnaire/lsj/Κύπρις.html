Eub.67.8; ἐν πλησμονῇ τοι Κ. Men.Mon.159, cf. B.Fr.16.4, E.Fr.951.     III. = sq., Eust.1574.24, Sch.Od.7.125. [ῠ by nature; in Ep. ῡ by position; never in Com., exc. in parodies.]
