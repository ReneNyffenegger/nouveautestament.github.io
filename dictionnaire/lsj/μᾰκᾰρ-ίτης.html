μᾰκᾰρ-ίτης [ῑ], ου, Dor. μᾰκᾰρί-τᾱς, ὁ, like μάκαρ III, one blessed, i.e. dead, esp. of one lately dead, A.Pers.633 (lyr.), Ar.Fr.488.10, Men.1032, PCair.Zen.447.1 (iii B.C.): freq. in late writers, Plu.2.120b, Ath.3.113e; ὁ μ. σου πατήρ your late father, Luc.DMeretr.6.1, etc.:—fem. μᾰκᾰρ-ῖτις, ιδος, Theoc.2.70, Herod.6.55; ἡ μ. μου γυνή Luc.Philops.27.     II. as Adj., μ. βίος, with a play on I, Ar.Pl.555.
