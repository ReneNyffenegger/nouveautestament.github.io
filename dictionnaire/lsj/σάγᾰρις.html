σάγᾰρις, εως Ion. ιος, ἡ; pl. σαγάρεις Ion. -ῑς:—a weapon used by the Scythian tribes, Hdt.1.215, 4.5; ἀξίνας σαγάρις εἶχον Id.7.64; by the Amazons, Aristarch. in PAmh.2.12 ii 10; by the Persians, Amazons, Mossynoeci, etc., X.An.4.4.16, 5.4.13:—acc. to Hsch. single-edged, and joined by X. with κοπίς and μάχαιρα, Cyr.1.2.9, 2.1.9, 4.2.22; double-edged acc. to AP6.94 (Phil.).
