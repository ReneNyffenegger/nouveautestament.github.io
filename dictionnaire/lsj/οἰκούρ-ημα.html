οἰκούρ-ημα, ατος, τό, the watch or keeping of a house, E.Hipp.787; οἰ. τῶνδε τῶν ξένων watch kept by these strangers (for οἱ οἰκουροῦντες ξένοι), S.Ph.868.     II. keeping the house, staying at home, E.Heracl.700.     2. in concrete sense, of persons, -ήματα φθείρειν corrupt the stay-at-homes, i.e. the women, Id.Or.928.
