φωσφορ-έω, bear or bring light, BGU597.33 (poet., i A. D.); shine, Nech. ap. Vett. Val.280.2, Ph.1.511, Man.1.65; esp. of the moon or planets, draw away more than 15o from the sun, rise, Heph.Astr.1.20; οἱ φωσφοροῦντες τόποι the positions of such risings in the zodiac, Ptol.Tetr.89.     2. trans., bring into the light, τὰ ἔμβρυα Olymp.in Alc.p.12 C.
