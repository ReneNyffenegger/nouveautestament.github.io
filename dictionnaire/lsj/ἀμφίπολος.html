ἀμφίπολος, ον, (πέλω, πολέω) busied about, busy, epith. of Κύπρις, S.Tr.860:—in Hom. and Hdt. only as fem. Subst., handmaid, waiting-woman, Od.1.331, 6.199, etc.; λάβετ’ ἀμφίπολοι γραίας ἀμενοῦς E.Supp.1115:—with other Substs., ἀ. ταμίη, γραῦς, Il.24.302, Od.1.191.     b. later, handmaid of gods, priestess, θεᾶς E.IT1114; Διός IG14.2111.     2. masc., attendant, follower, Pi.O.6.32.     3. masc., priest, sacrist, E.Fr.982; ὁ τῶν θεῶν ἀ. Phld.D.1.13, cf. Plu.Comp.Demetr.Ant.3, IG9(1).683 (Corcyra).     4. ἀ. Διὸς Ὀλυμπίου, title of magistrate at Syracuse, D.S.16.70.     II. in pass. sense, frequented, τύμβος Pi.O.1.93.
