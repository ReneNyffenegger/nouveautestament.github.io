περίπλοος, ον, contr. περί-πλους, gen. πλου, nom. pl. -πλοι:—circum-navigation, c. gen., τὸν π. τοῦ Ἄθω Hdt.6.95; περὶ Πελοπόννησον Th.2.80, cf. 8.4; τὸν π. τὸν εἰς Κέρκυραν Aeschin.3.243; esp. round the enemy's fleet, X.HG1.6.31: metaph., of a journey by land, Call.Fr.278; of the journey of the soul in transmigration, Diog.Oen.35.     II. account of a coasting voyage (opp. περίοδος of a land-journey), γράφειν τὸν π. τῆς ἔξω θαλάσσης Luc.Hist.Conscr.31: Periplus is the title of several geograph. works, still extant, by Scylax, Agatharchides, Arrian, etc.
