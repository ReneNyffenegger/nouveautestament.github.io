σφωΐτερος [ῐ], α, ον, possess. Adj. of σφῶϊ, Pron. of 2nd pers. dual, of you two, σ. ἔπος the word of you two, Hera and Athena, Il.1.216.     2. of σφωέ, Pron. of 3rd pers. dual, of them two or both of them, Antim.10,13.     II. = σφέτερος in A.R.:     1. for 2nd pers. pl., your own, your, 4.454.     2. for 2nd pers. sg., thine own, thine, thy, 3.395.     3. for 3rd pers. sg., his or her own, 2.465,544, al. (so Theoc.25.55); his or her, 1.643, 3.600.     4. for 3rd pers. pl., their own, 1.1286, Man.2.190.
