Τύχων [ῠ], ωνος, ὁ, (τύχη) a name of Hermes, Inscr.Magn.203 (iii B. C.), Hsch., Theognost.Can.33; of Priapus, D.S.4.6 (v.l. Τυφῶνα); [Πρίαπος] ἔοικε . . Τύχωνι Str.13.1.12; defined as δαίμων περὶ τὴν Ἀφροδίτην, Choerob.in Theod.1.274 H.; he is the giver of small gifts to mortals, AP9.334 (Pers.), cf. Apolloph.1 D.     2. name of the deified lance of Alexander of Pherae, Plu.Pel.29.
