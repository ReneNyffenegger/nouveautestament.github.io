Ὀρτῠγία, Ion. Ὀρτῠγ-ίη, ἡ, (ὄρτυξ) Quail-island, the ancient name of Delos or Rhenaea, Str.10.5.5; also of other places in Greece ; in one of these Artemis killed Orion, Od.5.123: hence she is called Ὀρτυγία, S.Tr.213 (lyr.).     II. part of the city of Syracuse, otherwise called Νᾶσος, the Island, Str.6.2.4.
