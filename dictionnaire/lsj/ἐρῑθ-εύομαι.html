ἐρῑθ-εύομαι, Dep., (ἔριθος) serve, work for hire, LXXTo.2.11:—so in Act., Hld.1.5.     II. of public officers or characters, canvass, intrigue for office, οἱ ἐριθευόμενοι Arist.Pol.1303a16; cf. ἐξεριθεύομαι.     2. later Act., generally, compete with, τινι Sch.S.Aj.833: abs., indulge in petty intrigue, Eust.1162.23.
