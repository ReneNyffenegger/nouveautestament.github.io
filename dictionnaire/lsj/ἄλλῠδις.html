ἄλλῠδις, Adv., (ἄλλος) Ep. for ἄλλοσε, elsewhither, in Hom. only with ἄλλος, ἄ. ἄλλος one hither, another thither, Il.11.486, Od.5.71, cf.A.R.2.980, etc.; τρέπεται χρὼς ἄ. ἄλλῃ his colour changes now one way, now another, Il.13.279; imitated from Hom. by Eup.159.11; later by itself, AP15.24.1 (Simm.).
