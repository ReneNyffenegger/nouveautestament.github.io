Ἀντίγονος, ὁ, name of several Macedonian kings:—hence Ἀντιγόνειος, α, ον, of Antigonus, Polyaen.4.9.1; Ἀντιγόνεια, τά, name of a festival in his honour, Plb.28.19.3, IG11.154A42 (Delos, iii B.C.): also Ἀντιγονικός, ή, όν, Plu.Arat.54:—fem. Ἀντιγονίς, ίδος, a kind of cup named from him, Polem.Hist.57, Plu.Aem.33:— Ἀντιγονίζω, to be on Antigonus' side, of his party, Polyaen.4.6.13, D.T.638.16.
