Poll.7.32, 10.125, EM344.1; and Hsch. cites νῶντα· νήθοντα; in Eup.l.c. Meineke restores νῆσαι for νῶσαι. (Cf. Lat. neo 'spin', OHG. nāan 'sew', etc.)
