λέμβ-ος, ὁ, a ship's cock-boat, D.32.6: metaph., of a parasite, ὄπισθεν ἀκολουθεῖ κόλαξ τῳ; λέμβος ἐπικέκληται Anaxandr.34.7.     II. fishing-boat, Theoc.21.12.     2. fast-sailing galley, felucca, used either to precede a fleet, Plb.1.53.9; or as a light transport, Id.2.3.1, cf. 5.109.3, SIG569.19 (Halasarna, iii B.C.), PPetr.2p.64 (iii B.C.).
