πῠροφόρος, ὁ, (πῦρ) in pl., inflammatory missiles, Ph.Bel.91.41, 94.8; engines which discharge such missiles, ib.95.20.     II. sg., bearer of sacrificial fire, at Epidaurus, IG42(1).400, al.; at Argos, SIG735.13 (i B.C.); written πουροφόρος, πυρωφώρος, IG5(1).997, 1021 (Laconia).     2. v.l. for πυρφόρος II.2b (q.v.).
