Phld.Rh.1.197 S.; one who delivers, recites, declaimer, ἐπῶν Tim.Lex. s.v. ῥαψῳδοί; rhapsodist, D.S.14.109, 15.7; this sense or sense II.1 is possible in PCair.Zen.4.44 (iii B. C.).     3. metaph., pretender, dissembler, hypocrite, LXXJb.34.30, 36.13, Ev.Matt.23.13, al.
