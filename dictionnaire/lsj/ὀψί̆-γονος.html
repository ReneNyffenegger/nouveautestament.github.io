ὀψί̆-γονος, ον, late-born, τί σευ ἄλλος ὀνήσεται ὀψίγονός περ; Il.16.31, cf. Thphr.Fr.30.6: mostly in pl., ὀψιγόνων ἀνθρώπων of men after-born, Il.3.353, cf. Od.1.302, etc.     2. of a son, late-born, born in one's old age, h.Cer.165, Arr.Ind.9.2; born late in the season, ἔριφος Longus4.6.     3. later-born, i.e. younger, A.Supp.361 (lyr.), Hdt.7.3; young, Theoc.24.31.     4. late in spawning, of the needle-fish, Arist.HA571a2.
