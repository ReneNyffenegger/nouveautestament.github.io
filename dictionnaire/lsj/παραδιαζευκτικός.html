παραδιαζευκτικός, ή, όν, σύνδεσμος subdisjunctive, i. e. disjunctive, with the meaning 'or if you like', used where either alternative alone and the two together are alike admissible, e.g. ἢ (ἠέ) in ἢ νέος ἠὲ παλαιός (Il.14.108), A.D.Conj.219.12, Gal.7.80, cf. Hdn.Gr.1.517, etc. Adv. παραδιαζευκτι-κῶς Gal.7.537, al.
