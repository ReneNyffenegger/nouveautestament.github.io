φάσηλος [ᾰ], ὁ (cf. Ath.2.56a, 4.139a: Lat. phaselos is fem., Colum.10.377), a kind of bean, calavance, Vigna sinensis, Epich.151, Ar.Pax1144 (troch.), Demetr.Com.Vet.5, Wilcken Chr.198.18 (iii B. C.), etc.; cf. φασίολος.     II. hence Lat. phaselus, a light boat, canoe, skiff, from its like ness to a bean-pod, Catull.4, Hor.Od.3.2.29.
