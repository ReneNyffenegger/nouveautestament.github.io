Λευκᾰθ-έα, ἡ, goddess worshipped in Thessaly ( = Λευκοθέα), IG 9(2).422 (Pherae), Ἀρχ. Ἐφ.1910.380 (Larissa).     II. neut. pl. Λευκάθεα, τά, festival at Teos, CIG3066.25:—hence Λευκᾰθ-εών, ῶνος (sc. μήν), ὁ, month at Chios, GDI5661.25 (iv B.C.); at Teos, Supp.Epigr. 2.579.21 (iv B.C.); at Magnesia on Maeander, Inscr.Magn.89.6:— also Λευκᾰθ-ιών, at Lampsacus, CIG3641b17; Λευκά̆θ-εος, at Astypalaea, SIG 780 (i B.C.).
