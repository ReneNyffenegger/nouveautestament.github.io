λᾱο-σσόος, ον, (σεύω) rousing or stirring the nations, epith. of the war-deities Ares, Eris, Il.17.398, 20.48; of Athena, 13.128, Od.22.210; of Apollo, Il.20.79: also of men, as Amphiaraus, Od.15.244; of Electryon, Amphitryon, Hes.Sc.3,37; λαοσσόοι ἀγῶνες assemblies to which the people flock, Pi.P.12.24.
