προσέλην-ος, ον, (σελήνη) before the moon, older than the moon, a name given to the Arcadians, as priding themselves on their antiquity, Arist.Fr.591, Hippys2, Plu.2.282a, Sch.A.R.4.264; expld. by other Gramm.as = ὑβριστικός (cf. προυσελέω), cf. EM690.11: ὁ προυσέληνος, = ὁ Ἀρκάς, Call.Iamb.1.121.     II. π. ἡμέραι the days before the new moon appears, Gp.1.6.2.
