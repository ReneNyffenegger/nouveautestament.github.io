ὀλολῡγ-ών, όνος, ἡ, croaking of the male frog, Arist.HA536a11, Ael.NA9.13; note of water-creatures, ib.6.19.     II. in Theoc.7.139, Arat.948, an unknown animal, evidently named from its note : some take it for a small owl, others for a singing bird, others again for the tree-frog ; cf. Eub.104, Thphr.Sign.42, AP5.291.5 (Agath.).
