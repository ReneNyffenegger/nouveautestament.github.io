Λοξ-ίας, ου, Ion. Λοξ-ίης, εω, ὁ, epith. of Apollo, B.12.148, Hdt.1.91, A.Eu.19,al., S.OT410, Ar.Eq.1047, Pl.8, etc.; expld. because the Sun traverses the ecliptic (cf.
