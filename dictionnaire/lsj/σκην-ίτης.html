σκην-ίτης [ῑ] (in codd. sts. misspelt σκηνήτης, which is accepted by Eust.70.29), ου, ὁ, dweller in tents or booths, of nomad tribes, Str.2.5.32, 11.2.1, etc.; one who keeps a stall, IG22.1672.15, 171, 7.2712.72 (Acraephia).     2. a low fellow, Isoc.17.33.     II. Adj. in or belonging to a tent, βίος D.S.2.40; κισσός AP7.36 (Eryc.).
