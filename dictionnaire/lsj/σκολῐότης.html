σκολῐότης, ητος, ἡ, crookedness, σ. τῆς καμπῆς, of a Parthian bow, Plu.Crass.24: in pl., the windings of a stream, Str.10.2.19; of windings generally, Id.12.8.15.     II. metaph., inequality, σκολιότητα ἔχειν to be unequally affected, Hp.Acut.(Sp.)22.     2. of men, crookedness, dishonesty, LXXEz.16.5.
