ἁλι-εύω, (ἅλς) fish, Ev.Jo.21.3; to be a fisher, Plu.Ant.29; fish for, catch, σπάρους dub. l. in Epich.54; λίθους Luc.Pisc.47: abs., Luc.Herm.65, etc.: metaph. of avenger, ἁλιεύειν τινάς LXXJe.16.16.     II. only Med. occurs in Att., Pl.Com.44; Ἁλιευομένη, title of play by Antiph.:—also later, Posidon.68.
