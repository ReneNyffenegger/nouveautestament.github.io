ὀρεω-κόμος, ὁ, (ὀρεύς) muleteer, Ar.Th.491,Fr.633,IG22.10B4 (v/iv B. C.), 1673.18 (iv B. C.), Pl.Ly.208b, X.HG5.4.42, Hyp.Lyc.5.—In codd. freq. misspelt ὀρεοκόμος, as in Pl.l.c., Poll.7.183, Hsch.; the latter also cites a form ὀρειοκόμος, which may be an Ep. spelling of *ὀρη(ϝ)οκόμος, the older form implied by ὀρεωκόμος.
