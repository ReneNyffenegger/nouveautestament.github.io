κομμός (A), ὁ, (κόπτω) striking; esp. beating of the head and breast in lamentation, ἔκοψα κομμὸν Ἄριον A.Ch.423 (lyr.), cf. Bion1.97 (pl.); hence,     2. in the Att. Drama, dirge, lament, sung alternately by one or more of the chief characters and the chorus, κ. δὲ θρῆνος κοινὸς χοροῦ καὶ ἀπὸ σκηνῆς Arist.Po.1452b24.
