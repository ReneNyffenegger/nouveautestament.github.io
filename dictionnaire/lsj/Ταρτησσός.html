Ταρτησσός, ὁ, Hdt.1.163, elsewh. ἡ:—a district of Spain at the mouth of the Baetis, the Tarshish of Scripture (Θαρσείς in LXX3 Ki.10.22, al.), Hdt. l.c., Arist.Mir.844a17, Str.3.2.11, etc.: also the river Baetis, Stesich.5 (ap.Str.l.c.):—hence Ταρτήσσιος, α, ον, Tartessian, Hdt.4.192, Ar.Ra.475; Ταρτήσσιοι, οἱ, Hdt.1.163, etc.
