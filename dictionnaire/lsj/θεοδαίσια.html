θεοδαίσια, τά, Cret. name for the Διονύσια, Call.Aet.Oxy.2080.88, GDI5075.43 (Crete), cf. Hsch.; also, at Rhodes, SIG1035c.     II. Θεοδαίσιος, ὁ, epith. of Dionysus, Hsch.; also, (sc. μήν) name of month found in various forms: Cret. θιοδαίσιος GDI5149; in Cos, Rhodes, etc., θευδαίσιος ib.3593, al., etc.; at Mytilene, θεδαίσιος IG11(4).1064a2 (Delos).
