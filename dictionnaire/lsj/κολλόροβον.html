κολλόροβον, τό, shepherd's staff or crook, BGU759.13 (ii A.D., written κολλώρ-οβον); applied to the so- called club of Orion and Bootes (which has this form), Hipparch.1.7.15, 2.6.1b, Ptol.Alm.7.5, 8.1.     II. masc. and neut., dub. sens., apptly. a weight or a coin, Sammelb.6954.     III. pl., v.l. for κιλλοβόροι in Poll.1.143.     IV. κολόροβοι, gloss on σκοιά, Hsch.; κολόροβον, gloss on κορύνη, Id.
