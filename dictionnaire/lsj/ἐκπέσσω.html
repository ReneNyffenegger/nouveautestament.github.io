ἐκπέσσω, Att. ἐκπέ-ττω, cook thoroughly: hence,     1. of animals, digest or concoct food thoroughly, Hp.VM22.     2. of plants, ripen, τὸν καρπόν Thphr.HP2.2.4:—Pass., of nourishment, to be assimilated, Arist.Col.799a11.     3. of eggs, hatch, Id.HA562b18 (Pass.), al.     4. ripen, bring to a head, of an abscess, Dsc.Eup.1.142.
