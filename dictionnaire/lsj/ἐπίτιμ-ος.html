ἐπίτιμ-ος, ον, of a citizen, in possession of his rights and franchises (τιμαί), opp. ἄτιμος (q.v.), Ar.Ra.702, And.1.73, Th.5.34, X.HG2.2.11, etc.; χρήματα ἐ. property not confiscated, though the owner was in exile, Lex ap.D.23.44.     II. valuable, Agath.1.8.     2. subject to penalty: hence, contraband, ἐλαϊκόν PTeb.39.10 (ii B.C.).     3. Subst. ἐπίτιμον, τό, = ἐπιτίμιον 2, SIG685.81 (pl., Crete, ii B.C.), PRev.Laws 43.8 (iii B.C.), Test.Epict.6.31, PGen.1.20.15 (ii B.C.), etc.
