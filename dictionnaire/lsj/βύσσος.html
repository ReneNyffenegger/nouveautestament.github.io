βύσσος, ἡ, flax, and the linen made from it, Emp.93, Theoc.2.73, etc.; used of perennial flax, Linum angustifolium, grown in Elis, Paus.6.26.6, and of Linum usitatissimum, = λίνον Ἑβραίων, Id.5.5.2; also, in later writers, of Indian cotton, Gossypium herbaceum, Poll.7.76, Philostr.VA2.20; and of silk, τὰ Σηρικὰ ἔκ τινων φλοιῶν ξαινομένης βύσσου Str.15.1.20.
