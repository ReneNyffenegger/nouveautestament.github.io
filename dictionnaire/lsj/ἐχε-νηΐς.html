ἐχε-νηΐς, ΐδος, contr. ἐχε-νῇς, ῇδος, ἡ, (ναῦς) ship- detaining, ἄπλοιαι A.Ag.149 (lyr.); ἄγκυρα AP6.27.5 (Theaet.); γαλήνη Nonn.D.13.114.     II. a small fish, supposed to have the power of holding ships back, Arist.HA505b19, Opp.H.1.212, Plin.HN9.79; in form ἐχεναΐς, = Lat. remora, Donat.ad Ter.Andr. 739, Eun.302.
